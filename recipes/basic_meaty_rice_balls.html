Basic Meaty Rice Balls

Description

Meaty Rice Balls are another good starting recipe. As with all meat recipes, higher quality meat will result in more health restored!

It's also possible to add additional effects to the recupe. Adding a Stamella Shroom to the recipe creates Energizing Meaty Rice Balls, which also restores Link's Stamina Wheel!

Ingredients

  • 1st Ingredient: Hylian Rice
  • 2nd Ingredient: Meat

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients you need
  2. find a cooking pot, which can usually be found close to towns and stables
  3. Go to your inventory to hold all the ingredients
  4. Get close to the cooking pot and press the A-button when the Cook option appears
